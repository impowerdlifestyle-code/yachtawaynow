Why Clearwater

Clearwater's Best Kept Secret

Clearwater Beach is consistently ranked among the top beaches in the United States — and the best way to experience it is from the water. Whether you're looking for a private boat rental in Clearwater or a full-service yacht charter, we give you access to the most breathtaking stretches of Clearwater's coastline, far from the crowded shore.

Departing from our St. Petersburg dock, it's a short and scenic cruise north through the Intracoastal Waterway to reach Clearwater's turquoise waters. Along the way, you'll pass stunning waterfront estates, pristine mangrove islands, and the iconic Clearwater Pass — the gateway to the Gulf of Mexico's open waters.

Clearwater Beach Access

Cruise directly to Clearwater Beach and anchor offshore for swimming, sunbathing, and unmatched Gulf views.

Caladesi Island

Visit one of Florida's last unspoiled barrier islands — only accessible by boat. White sand, clear water, and total seclusion.

Sand Key

Anchor at Sand Key for a quiet escape just south of Clearwater Pass. Pristine sands, gentle surf, and unbeatable sunsets.

Clearwater Pass

Cruise through Clearwater Pass into the open Gulf of Mexico. Perfect for dolphin sightings and breathtaking sunset runs.

Destinations

Where We'll Take You

From our St. Petersburg dock, Clearwater's finest destinations are just a cruise away. Here's what awaits you on the water.

Clearwater Beach

America's top-rated beach is even more stunning from the water. Anchor offshore, swim in crystal-clear Gulf waters, and take in panoramic views of the Clearwater skyline. The sugar-white sand stretches for miles.

Caladesi Island State Park

One of the few completely natural barrier islands left in Florida. Accessible only by boat, Caladesi offers untouched beaches, nature trails, and a kayak trail through mangrove tunnels. A true hidden paradise.

Sand Key & The Gulf

Sand Key sits just south of Clearwater Pass, offering calm waters and a serene atmosphere perfect for anchoring. From here, head through the pass into the open Gulf for dolphin encounters and legendary sunsets.
