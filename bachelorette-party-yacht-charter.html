Sample Itinerary

Your Bachelorette Day on the Water

Every charter is customized to your group, but here's what a typical 5-hour weekend bachelorette looks like. We'll work with you to build the perfect itinerary.

2:30 PM — Boarding & Setup

Arrive at our St. Petersburg marina. The crew helps load drinks, food, and decorations. Get settled, take your first group photos on the flybridge.

3:00 PM — Cruise & Celebrate

Head out into Tampa Bay. Music up, drinks flowing, sun shining. Cruise past the St. Pete waterfront and out toward the Gulf islands. Anchor for a swim if you'd like.

6:00 PM — Golden Hour & Sunset

The magic hour. Position the yacht for the perfect Gulf sunset. Golden light, champagne toasts, and the kind of photos that end up framed. This is the moment.

7:30 PM — Return to Marina

Cruise back as the city lights come on. Dock, collect your belongings, and head out to continue the night in downtown St. Pete — just minutes away.
