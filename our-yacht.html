Overview

What Is the 52ft Marquis Flybridge Like?

The Marquis Flybridge is one of the most respected names in luxury yachting. Built by Marquis Yachts in Pulaski, Wisconsin, this 52-footer combines American craftsmanship with serious offshore capability. With over 2,000 square feet of total living space spread across three distinct levels, she offers more room than most charter guests expect — and more comfort than they imagined possible on the water.

Whether you are hosting a celebration for 13 guests or enjoying a quiet afternoon for two, every corner of this yacht has been designed for comfort, style, and function.

Deck by Deck

Explore Every Level

Three distinct decks, each with its own character. Here is what you will find aboard.

Main Deck

The heart of the yacht. A spacious open-plan salon with panoramic windows offers unobstructed views of the Gulf. The full galley kitchen sits forward — equipped with a refrigerator, stove, microwave, and ample counter space for catering setups. The aft deck is where most of the action happens: a shaded outdoor lounge area with wraparound seating, a dining table, and direct access to the swim platform for water activities.

Lower Cabin

Below deck, you will find the private quarters. The master stateroom features a full-size bed, en-suite head with shower, and ample storage. A second guest cabin offers additional sleeping accommodations for overnight charters and multi-day Bahamas trips. Both cabins are fully climate-controlled with individual AC units, ensuring comfort regardless of the Florida heat. Two full restrooms serve guests throughout the charter.

Flybridge

The crown jewel. The upper flybridge deck is the ultimate spot for sunset views, open-air lounging, and soaking in the Gulf Coast scenery. A secondary helm station allows the captain to operate from above while guests enjoy the main deck. The flybridge features its own seating area, sun pads for lounging, and is the best vantage point on the yacht — perfect for spotting dolphins, watching the Skyway Bridge light up, or simply enjoying the breeze.
