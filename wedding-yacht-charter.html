Why a Yacht Wedding

An Unforgettable Beginning

There is something about the open water that strips away everything except what matters. No crowded venue, no rigid timelines, no strangers at the next table. Just you, your closest people, and the Gulf of Mexico stretching out to the horizon as the sun paints the sky in gold and rose.

A yacht wedding or proposal is the kind of moment people talk about for decades. The gentle sway of the water beneath you, a warm breeze carrying the scent of salt air, and that breathtaking moment when the sun dips below the waterline right as you exchange vows or drop to one knee. It does not get more cinematic than that.

Our 52ft Marquis Flybridge gives you the intimacy of a private celebration with the grandeur of the Gulf Coast as your venue. Whether you are planning a micro-wedding for your closest family and friends or a surprise proposal she will never see coming, we have hosted dozens of couples and know exactly how to make your day seamless.

Ceremonies at Sea

Intimate Ceremonies on the Water

A yacht wedding is the ultimate micro-wedding. With space for up to 13 guests across three beautifully appointed decks, every person present is someone who truly matters to you. There are no filler seats, no obligatory invites, no table assignments for distant relatives you have not seen in a decade. This is your inner circle, gathered in one of the most stunning settings on the Gulf Coast.

Your officiant is welcome aboard, and many couples choose to have the ceremony on the flybridge with panoramic views of the bay. The climate-controlled main cabin provides a comfortable gathering area before and after the ceremony, while the aft deck and swim platform offer additional space for mingling, toasting, and taking photos against the water.

We work with you to plan every detail. Want to anchor near Egmont Key for a ceremony with the historic lighthouse in the background? We can do that. Prefer to exchange vows while slowly cruising past the St. Pete waterfront as the city lights flicker on? That is equally beautiful. The water is your venue, and the possibilities are as wide as the horizon.

Pop the Question

Sunset Proposals

Planning a proposal is one of the most nerve-wracking and exciting things you will ever do. You want it to be perfect, private, and completely surprising. A yacht proposal checks every box. She thinks you are just going on a sunset cruise. She has no idea that the champagne is already chilled, the flowers are arranged on the flybridge, and the crew is standing by to capture the moment.

Our crew has helped dozens of partners pull off flawless surprise proposals. We will coordinate the timing so you are positioned perfectly for golden hour. We can arrange for rose petals, a custom playlist queued to your song, and champagne ready to pop the second she says yes. If you want friends and family hiding below deck for a surprise reveal afterward, we have done that too.

The privacy of a yacht means no awkward bystanders, no waiters interrupting the moment, and no worrying about other people's reactions. It is just the two of you, the water, and one of the most beautiful sunsets in the country. After the proposal, cruise the bay together, toast with champagne, and let the magnitude of the moment sink in surrounded by nothing but sky and sea.

Sample Itinerary

Your Wedding Day on the Water

Every wedding charter is fully customized, but here is what a typical 5-hour weekend celebration looks like. We will work with you and your officiant to build the perfect timeline.

3:00 PM — Boarding & Final Touches

Arrive at our St. Petersburg marina. The crew helps with final decoration setup, arranges flowers, and ensures everything is picture-perfect. Guests board and settle in with welcome drinks.

3:30 PM — Cast Off & Cruise

Depart the marina and cruise into the bay. Guests enjoy the flybridge and aft deck as you pass the St. Pete waterfront. Music plays softly as anticipation builds.

5:30 PM — Ceremony & Champagne Toast

Anchor in a scenic location as golden hour begins. The ceremony takes place on the flybridge with the sunset as your backdrop. Champagne corks pop. Tears flow. Everyone cheers.

6:00 PM — Sunset Reception & Photos

Cruise slowly as the sun sets. Your photographer captures portraits with the Gulf as your backdrop. Enjoy dinner, cake, and dancing on the water. Return to the marina by 8:00 PM glowing.
