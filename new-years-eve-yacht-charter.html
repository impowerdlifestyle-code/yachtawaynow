Welcome the New Year

New Year's Eve Yacht Charter — Ring It In on the Water

Skip the crowded rooftop bars and overpriced restaurant prix fixe menus. Welcome the new year on the open water with your closest friends — champagne toast, city skyline, fireworks from every angle.

The Ultimate NYE

Why NYE on a Yacht?

Every year, millions of people pack into the same overpriced restaurants, the same overcrowded bars, and the same house parties where you spend half the night looking for a parking spot and the other half trying to get a drink at the bar. New Year's Eve is supposed to be special. It is the one night of the year when everyone agrees to stop, take stock, and celebrate the turn of the calendar together. So why spend it shoulder-to-shoulder with strangers?

A private yacht charter on Tampa Bay gives you the New Year's Eve you actually want. Your closest friends. Your music on the premium stereo. Your champagne chilled in the galley. Three decks of space to spread out, dance, lounge, and count down to midnight under the stars. When the clock strikes twelve, you are standing on the flybridge with a 360-degree view of fireworks launching from Tampa, St. Petersburg, and Clearwater simultaneously. No craning your neck. No blocked views. Just the entire sky on fire, reflected on the black water around you.

And the best part? No noise ordinances, no last call, no surge pricing on rideshares at 1am. You are on the water on your own schedule. BYOB means you bring the good champagne — the Dom, the Veuve, whatever you have been saving for a special occasion. This is that occasion. The captain handles the navigation. You handle the celebration.

The Experience

The NYE Experience

Your New Year's Eve charter is entirely customizable, but here is what the night typically looks like aboard our 52ft Marquis Flybridge. From the moment you step on board, the energy shifts. The marina lights reflect off the water, the music starts, and you realize this is going to be a New Year's Eve unlike any you have experienced before.

Boarding begins at 8pm at our St. Petersburg marina. The yacht is prepped and ready — climate-controlled interior, premium sound system queued up, coolers stocked with ice. Bring your food, your drinks, your party favors, and your best outfit. As you cast off, the city skyline slides into view and the night begins.

The captain cruises the Tampa Bay waterfront, passing the gleaming downtown skylines of both St. Pete and Tampa. The water is calm, the air is warm by mainland standards, and the lights of the bridges and buildings create a backdrop that no rooftop bar can match. Dinner on the aft deck, drinks on the flybridge, dancing in the main cabin — however you want to spend the evening, the yacht is yours.

As midnight approaches, we anchor in a prime viewing location on Tampa Bay. The countdown happens on the flybridge with champagne glasses raised. When the clock hits zero, fireworks erupt from multiple directions — Tampa, St. Pete, and the beaches all launch simultaneously, and you are positioned right in the center of it all. Toast the new year, hug your people, and soak in a fireworks show that surrounds you on every side. The return cruise to the marina is around 1am, giving you time to relive the night while the city skyline fades behind you.
