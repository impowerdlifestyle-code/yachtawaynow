What Kind of Yacht Does Yacht Away Now Operate?

Yacht Away Now operates a 52ft Marquis Flybridge — a luxury three-deck yacht that accommodates up to 13 guests. Whether it's a sunset cruise, bachelorette party, or a group of best friends living it up, she's the right boat for the best day on the water.

She has 3 levels of space — over 1,200 sq ft of cabin space and over 800 sq ft of outside deck and bridge area. Powered by High Output 1320hp engines moving at over 30 knots! With a 600 mile range, trips to the Keys are a weekend adventure.

Multiple yachts available upon request. Our flagship 52ft Marquis Flybridge is our most popular vessel, but we have additional yachts in our fleet to accommodate your needs. Contact us to discuss options.
