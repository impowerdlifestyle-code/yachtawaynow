Well Played Charters — Critical Issues

These issues are actively suppressing their search visibility:

F1. robots.txt blocks /wp-content/ — images, CSS, and JS hidden from Google
Google cannot render their pages properly. This is the single most damaging SEO issue on the site. All images are stored in /wp-content/uploads/ which is blocked from crawling.
F2. Schema data conflict — operating hours show 9am-5pm in code, 8am-10pm on page
Google may display incorrect business hours in search results and Maps, eroding trust and causing missed bookings.
F3. Key location URL has "peterburg" typo (missing a letter)
Their most important geo-targeted page misspells the city name in the URL, missing exact-match keyword searches.
F4. Blog author is "adms" — WordPress admin username, not a real person
Google's E-E-A-T guidelines heavily weight named human authorship. An admin username signals low-effort or auto-generated content.
F5. "As Seen On" section uses paid press release syndication, not earned media
Presenting paid distribution as media coverage is misleading and could trigger Google's link spam penalties.
F6. All 22 blog posts are about bachelor/bachelorette parties only
Zero content covering weddings, corporate events, family charters, sunset cruises, or destinations — despite offering all these services. Massive keyword gap.
