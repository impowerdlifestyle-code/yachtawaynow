Destinations

Where We'll Take You

Tampa Bay is your playground. Here are some of the most popular destinations for our private boat charters.

The Pier & Downtown St. Pete

Cruise past the iconic St. Pete Pier and the stunning downtown waterfront. Watch the city skyline glow during golden hour and enjoy panoramic views of Tampa Bay from the water.

Egmont Key & Shell Key

Anchor at the pristine sandbars of Shell Key or explore historic Egmont Key with its lighthouse and Civil War fort. Swim, snorkel, and enjoy untouched island beaches accessible only by boat.

Fort De Soto & The Gulf

Head south past the Skyway Bridge to Fort De Soto Park, one of America's top-rated beaches. Anchor in crystal-clear waters and spend the day swimming, shelling, and soaking in the Gulf Coast sun.
