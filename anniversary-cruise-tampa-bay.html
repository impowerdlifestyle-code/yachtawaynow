Why Choose Us

An Anniversary Worth Remembering

There's no better way to celebrate your milestone than aboard a private yacht, watching the sun set over the Gulf Coast with the person you love. Whether it's your 1st anniversary or your 50th, our 52ft Marquis Flybridge provides the intimate, luxurious setting your celebration deserves — complete with three spacious decks, over 1,200 sq ft of cabin space, and 800+ sq ft of open deck.

Bring your own champagne, wine, and favorite foods — we're fully BYOB. Many couples arrange a private dining setup on the flybridge or aft deck for the ultimate romantic atmosphere. The golden hour on Tampa Bay is truly something special, with the sun painting the sky in shades of amber and pink as you cruise past the waterfront. Perfect for proposals, vow renewals, and every milestone in between.
