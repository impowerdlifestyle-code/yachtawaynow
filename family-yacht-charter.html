On the Water

Family-Friendly Activities

Tampa Bay is a natural playground. Here is what your family can experience aboard our yacht.

01

Dolphin Watching

Tampa Bay is home to a resident population of bottlenose dolphins. Our captain knows exactly where to find them, and they frequently swim right alongside the yacht. Kids absolutely light up when dolphins appear just feet away from the boat.

02

Island Hopping

Anchor at Shell Key for pristine white sand beaches, or visit Egmont Key to explore the historic fort and lighthouse. These barrier islands are only accessible by boat, so you will have uncrowded beaches perfect for shelling, wading, and building sandcastles.

03

Swimming & Snorkeling

Our swim platform makes getting in and out of the water easy for all ages. Anchor in a calm, shallow spot and let the kids splash around. The clear Gulf waters are perfect for spotting fish, sea stars, and the occasional friendly manatee.

Comfort for Everyone

Kid-Friendly Amenities

We know that a great family day on the water requires more than just a beautiful boat. It requires thoughtful details that keep everyone comfortable, no matter their age. The shaded flybridge is perfect for little ones who need a break from the sun while still enjoying the views. The enclosed, air-conditioned main cabin provides a cool retreat for nap time, snack time, or simply a break from the elements.

The full galley kitchen has a refrigerator, microwave, and plenty of counter space for preparing meals or storing provisions. The complete bathroom means no stressful situations with young children. And because we are BYOB, you bring exactly what your family loves to eat and drink. Pack juice boxes, goldfish crackers, fruit platters, or order a pizza delivered to the marina before departure. There are no overpriced resort menus here.

The swim platform at the stern sits just above the waterline, making it easy and safe for kids to wade in and climb out. When anchored in shallow water near an island, the whole family can step right off the boat and onto the beach. Many families tell us this is the part their children talk about for months afterward — the freedom of having an entire island beach practically to themselves.

Sample Itinerary

Your Family Day on the Water

Every family charter is customized to your group, but here is what a typical full-day adventure looks like. We tailor the pace, stops, and activities to your family's ages and interests.

9:00 AM — Morning Departure

Board at our St. Petersburg marina. Load coolers, snacks, and beach gear. The crew gives a quick safety briefing and the kids meet the captain. Cast off into the calm morning waters of Tampa Bay.

10:00 AM — Dolphin Spotting

Cruise through known dolphin corridors. The captain slows down as pods appear. Kids line up along the rail, cameras ready. Dolphins often swim within arm's reach of the bow.

11:30 AM — Island Beach Time

Anchor at Shell Key or Egmont Key. Step off the swim platform onto white sand. Explore tide pools, collect shells, build sandcastles. Lunch aboard the yacht whenever everyone is hungry.

2:00 PM — Swim Stop & Return

One final swim stop in calm, shallow water. Kids play on the swim platform while adults relax on the flybridge. Cruise back to the marina, sun-kissed and already planning your next trip.
