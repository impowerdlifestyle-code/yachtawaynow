Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about chartering with Yacht Away Now.

Our 52ft Marquis Flybridge accommodates up to 13 guests comfortably across 3 decks with over 1,200 sq ft of cabin space and 800+ sq ft of outdoor deck.
Every charter includes 2 cabins and 3 decks, premium high-quality stereo, and a professional crew. Captain and fuel are additional for hourly charters, and included in weekend packages.
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. Last-minute availability may be possible on weekdays — call (727) 609-2248 to check.
Yes! You are welcome to bring your own food, beverages, and decorations aboard. We can also recommend local catering options for a fully catered experience.
Sunset cruises typically run 4-5 hours, departing in the late afternoon to catch the stunning Gulf Coast sunset. Timing varies by season.
Yes! Our 52ft Marquis Flybridge has a 600-mile range and is fully equipped for multi-day Bahamas and Florida Keys trips. These start at $5,000+ and include full cabin accommodations.
For weekend packages ($2,000/$2,500), the captain and crew are included. For hourly charters ($300/hr), there is an additional $100/hr captain fee and $50/hr fuel surcharge.
Safety is our top priority. The captain monitors weather conditions and makes the final call. If conditions are unsafe, we will work with you to reschedule your charter at no additional charge.
All charters depart from our home marina on 38th Way S in St. Petersburg, Florida. It is centrally located on Tampa Bay with easy access to the Gulf of Mexico, Shell Key, and Egmont Key.
Yes. Our 52ft Marquis Flybridge has 2 full heads (restrooms) located in the lower cabin, so guests always have convenient access during the charter.
Absolutely. BYOB is welcome aboard with no corkage or service fees. Bring your favorite wine, beer, or cocktails and enjoy them on the water at your leisure.
Tampa Bay is a year-round charter destination with an average of 361 sunny days per year. October through May is the most popular season with mild temperatures and low humidity.
Yes! We offer custom wedding and proposal packages tailored to your vision. From intimate proposals at sunset to full wedding celebrations on the water, contact us to plan your special day.
There is no minimum age for guests. Children and families are welcome aboard. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian, and the person booking must be 21 or older.
Yes! Our yacht has a swim platform for easy water access. Popular stops include Shell Key and nearby sandbars where guests can swim, snorkel, and explore the crystal-clear Gulf waters.
We appreciate your service. Please contact us directly at (727) 609-2248 or through our website to inquire about special pricing for military members and first responders.
Sunset times in St. Petersburg vary by season, roughly between 6:30 PM and 8:30 PM. We time our sunset cruise departures to ensure you experience the full golden hour on the water.
Weekday charters start at $300/hr with a 4-hour minimum. Weekend packages range from $2,000 to $2,500. Bahamas and multi-day trips start at $5,000+. Call for a custom quote.
Our flagship is the 52ft Marquis Flybridge with 3 decks and over 1,200 sq ft of cabin space. Additional yachts are available upon request for larger parties or special events.
Yes! The yacht is equipped with a premium high-quality stereo system with Bluetooth connectivity. Simply connect your phone and play your own playlists throughout the charter.
We operate BYOB — bring whatever food and drinks you like with no extra fees. We can also recommend trusted local catering partners to handle everything for a fully catered experience.
Booking is easy. Call us at (727) 609-2248 or fill out the booking form at yachtawaynow.com/contact. We'll confirm availability, discuss your preferences, and lock in your date.
