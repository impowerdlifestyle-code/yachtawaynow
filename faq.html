Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about chartering with Yacht Away Now.

Our 62ft Princess Flybridge accommodates up to 13 guests comfortably across 3 decks with over 1,200 sq ft of cabin space and 800+ sq ft of outdoor deck.
Every charter includes full access to all 3 decks, premium sound system, and a professional crew. Captain and fuel are additional for hourly charters, and included in weekend packages.
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. Last-minute availability may be possible on weekdays — call (727) 609-2248 to check.
Full refund up to 14 days prior to your charter date. Cancellations within 14 days may be subject to a partial charge. We understand plans change and work with you whenever possible.

Sunset cruises typically run 4-5 hours, departing in the late afternoon to catch the stunning Gulf Coast sunset. Timing varies by season.
