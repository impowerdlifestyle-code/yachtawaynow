Departing Directly from St. Petersburg

St. Petersburg is our home — and your gateway to the best water experiences on Florida's Gulf Coast. Whether you're searching for a charter boat in St. Pete or a luxury day on the water, our 52ft Marquis Flybridge is docked right here, which means no transfers, no waiting, and no wasted time. You board steps from downtown and you're on the water in minutes.

From the iconic St. Pete Pier and the historic Vinoy to the pristine beaches of Pass-a-Grille, our St. Petersburg yacht charter puts you at the center of it all. Cruise past the stunning downtown waterfront, watch dolphins play in Tampa Bay, and experience the Sunshine City from the best vantage point — the water.
