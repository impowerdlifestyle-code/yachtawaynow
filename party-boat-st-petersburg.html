Not Your Average Party Boat

Forget the overcrowded, impersonal party boats packed with strangers. When you book with Yacht Away Now, you get a private 52ft Marquis Flybridge all to yourself. Whether you're planning a bachelorette party, birthday celebration, anniversary, engagement, or just a night out with friends, our yacht provides the perfect backdrop. Cruise the stunning St. Petersburg waterfront as the sun sets over Tampa Bay, dance on the flybridge under the stars, and create memories that last a lifetime. Your professional captain and crew handle everything so you can focus on the party.

Premium Sound System
